SIDOMULYO

VILLAGE

On the outskirts of Batu City, Sidomulyo village has often faced issues for women and children related to poverty and low levels of education for many of its residents.

By engaging women and youth, the Peace Village programme focused towards directly protecting women and children, while also approaching the causes of community conflict, including working with households to generate income rather than taking loans.
